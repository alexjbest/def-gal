\(k\) finite field of characteristic \(p\text{.}\) \(\mathcal C_k\) category of complete local Noetherian rings with a fixed identification

\begin{equation*} A/\ideal m_A \simeq k \end{equation*}

with \((A, \ideal m_A)\) a local ring, maps are local homomorphisms preserving the fixed identification.
