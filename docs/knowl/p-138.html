Universal property: framed case:

\begin{equation*} \exists \rho _{univ}^{\square} \colon G \to \GL_d(R_{\bar \rho }^\square) \end{equation*}

s.t. if \(\rho _A\) is a lift of \(\bar \rho \) to \(A \) then there exists a unique

\begin{equation*} R_{\bar \rho }^\square \xrightarrow\phi A \end{equation*}

which is in \(\mathcal C\) s.t.

\begin{equation*} \xymatrix{ G \ar[r]^{\rho_{univ}^\square } \ar[dr]_{\rho _A}& \GL_d(A)\ar[d]^{\phi ^*}\\ & \GL_d(A) } \end{equation*}
