A second dimension is from \(p\)-adic variation, given \(f \) of weight \(k_0\) there exists

\begin{equation*} \mathscr F = \sum_{n\ge1} a_n(x) q^n \end{equation*}

where \(a_n(x)\) is a \(p\)-adic analytic function on a disc around \(k_0\) s.t.

\begin{equation*} k \in \ZZ^{\ge 2} \end{equation*}

in that disc.

\begin{equation*} \mathscr F_k = \sum _{n\ge 1} a_n(k) q^n \in S_k(\Gamma _0(Np)) \end{equation*}

an eigenform.

\begin{equation*} \rho _{\mathscr F} \colon G_{\QQ,S} \to \GL_2(A) \end{equation*}

where \(A\) are analytic functions in a disk.
