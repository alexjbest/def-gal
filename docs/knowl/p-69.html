\begin{equation*} f = \sum a_nq^n \in S_2(\Gamma _0(N), \QQ) \end{equation*}

a weight 2 eigenform on \(\Gamma _0(N)\text{,}\) with rational fourier coefficients. Eichler-Shimura gives \(E_f/\QQ\) an elliptic curve. Define

\begin{equation*} \rho _f = \rho _{E,p^\infty }\colon G_\QQ \to \GL_2(\QQ_p) \end{equation*}

unramified outside \(Np\)

\begin{equation*} \trace(\rho (\Frob_\ell)) = a_\ell \end{equation*}

for \(\ell\nmid Np\text{.}\) More generally

\begin{equation*} f \in S_2(\Gamma _0(N)) \end{equation*}

an eigenform, Eichler-Shimura gives

\begin{equation*} A_f/\QQ \end{equation*}

an abelian variety which leads to

\begin{equation*} \rho _f \colon G_\QQ \to \GL_2(K) \end{equation*}

\(K/\QQ_p\) finite.
