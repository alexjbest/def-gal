Deformation functor.
\begin{equation*} \bar \rho \colon G \to \GL_d(k) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} D_{\bar \rho} \colon \mathcal C_k \to \Set \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} A \mapsto \{\text{deformations of }\bar \rho \text{ to } A\} = \{\rho \colon G \to \GL_d(A) \text{ lifting }\bar \rho \}/\text{strict equiv.}\text{.} \end{equation*}

Given

\begin{equation*} R\in \mathcal C_k \end{equation*}

we have

\begin{equation*} F_R \colon \mathcal C_k \to \Set \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} A \mapsto \Hom (R, A)\text{.} \end{equation*}
